Color Convertor App

This app will take a know color number and convert it in to a different color format.

Simply input the known color type and value.

*note:
  • For RGB values, enter numbers with comma separators : eg. 255,255,255
  • For HEX values, enter numbers only, no # sign : eg. ffffff
  • For HSL values, enter numbers only, with comma separators (no % signs) : eg. 180,50,50
