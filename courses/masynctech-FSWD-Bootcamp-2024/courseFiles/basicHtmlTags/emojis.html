Entities

Entities are "special characters" such as space/less than/ greater than/dollar sign

These must be entered using the special codes that start with &. You can
get codes from several sites online such as Character Entity Reference Chart

Examples:

Dollar Sign is: & followed by "dollar;" | $

These can also be modified by the font styling

Dollar Sign is: & followed by "dollar;" | $
Less Than is: & followed by "lt;" | <
Asterisk is: & followed by "ast;" | *
